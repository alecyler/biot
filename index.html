Build creatures. Watch them survive (or die). Start simple. Add predators later.

Mobile tip: portrait is great for building and steering. Landscape is calmer when you mostly want to watch the tank.

  • Biots evolve, hunt, and die on their own.
  • Use the builder to make species and throw them into the food web.
  • Start with flowers, then add hunters once the world has food.

How to play + what you are seeing

Good first move: spawn a few flower types, let the world stabilize, then add hunters.

Builder: click segment chips or endpoints in the preview, change type, angle, and length, then spawn.

Inspector: click any biot in the world to inspect it and save survivors back into the builder.

Legend: green/photo feeds on light, red/predator bites, blue/propulsion moves, gold/reproduction lays eggs, gray/structure supports, silver/armor hardens.

Build ideas: structure + photo makes dependable flowers; perception + brain + propulsion makes rovers; launcher/lightning/flame/frost shine once food is stable.

Hidden-feeling tricks: glow boosts light harvesting in dim spots but also increases visibility; camo does the reverse. Advanced segments wake up when a biot reaches maturity.
